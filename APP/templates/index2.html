Why Chose Us?

Startup Coach is a free, open-source platform which aims to connect software developers and mentors all over the world. It is built by a group of talented and passionate developers, designers, engineers, and humans who want to make the engineering world a better place to collaborate. This project was born out of a desire to provide a platform to connect mentors and mentees throughout the world at no cost. Coding Coach is created by the people, for the people.
